Who we are

At Rehab Africa, we work towards creating that safe place for your well being. We achieve this by walking with you through the path to wholesome recovery. This is a platform where the sun shines brightly and hopes come alive; a place where happiness is expressed so that you want to dance on your feet and bring to life those beautiful colours within; Broken crayons they say, color beautifully; in fact they colour the best. In this community bonded by love; there abounds so much to tap from; courses to brighten your day, songs to make you dance and a community of people who have walked this path; what better way to learn than from those who have conquered their demons. Our therapies include but not limited to life skills, suicide de-structuring, anger management and relapse prevention. Bonus: While you are at it, you get certified on technical skills which would range from tech, culinary skills, people management..just name it! These therapies and course sessions specifically tailored to meet your needs will impact on your behavioural approach when it comes to dealing with specific triggers that makes life unwholesome for you. Your worries are our concern; we aim to achieve a better Africa; You and I. .

About

We are a coalition of young African ladies that believe so much in healing and that everyone deserves wholeness. We cut through the boundaries of Ethiopia, Nigeria, Cameroon,Kenya,Mali... We are passionate about a healthy Africa and a healthy Africa is you and I.
